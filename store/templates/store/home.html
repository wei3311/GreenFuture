MISSION

Our Mission

GreenFuture is on a quest to help make sustainable, earth-friendly commerce the new normal.

As consumers, we can influence real change by making environmentally sustainable purchases – but we know that it can be hard to find Earth friendly products that deliver what they promise.

That’s why we created GreenFuture – to empower you to take action with products you can trust.

Protect

We motivate people on protecting our beautiful Earth and give our children a better future. Together, we can align our actions with our values to create positive environmental change.

Promote

We inspire conscious commerce by offering trusted, high quality goods that create authentic positive impact. Sustainable product can be can be cheap in price, expensive in value

Perform

Together, we aim to learn, share, and celebrate a healthier planet. With your help, we’re forming a community that’s capable of wide-reaching impact.

OUR FOUNDER

Michelle Teoh Jia Yeong

“I've often said that we are doing something far more important than just selling products;
We are changing lives. Not even ours, but also our children.”

Inspiration

For Michelle, it was always mission possible. After experiencing a diving trip in Pulau Tioman, Pahang, she knew she had to make a change, not only for herself but for the Earth.

During the trip, she understood that currently we are facing a huge environment crisis and our sealifes are the one suffering alot. And this is how the ideas of developing eco-business in Malaysia society came out

Journey

2018 - Michelle Teoh actively participate in various of environment talk and eco-business sharings to have more understand about the circle.
2019 - launched her eco-merchants business -- GreenFuture Store in Bukit Jalil, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
2020 - Winning the Best Young Enterprise Award, bringing the brand's reputation to a wider range of places
